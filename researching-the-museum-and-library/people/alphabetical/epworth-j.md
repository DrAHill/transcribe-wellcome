



# Epworth J.


"Mr J. Epworth."  

Pseudonym used by [C.J.S. Thompson](Thompson, Charles John Samuel) when purchasing items.  The pseudonymous company name "[Epworth & Co.)" was also used.

Documented in receipts for purchasing books for WHML in
WA/HMM/CO/Chr
WA/HMM/CO/Aba
WA/HMM/LI/Bks

for example, in 1928: WA/HMM/CO/Chr/C/16 image 0161
